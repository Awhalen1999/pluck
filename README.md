## Why I made this

When Iâ€™m designing, Iâ€™m constantly switching between my editor, browser tabs, and random screenshots to reference a design.

It breaks focus fast and is annoying to be constantly looking around for what I need.

Pluck is meant to solve that by letting you keep inspiration on screen instead of hidden behind tabs.

## How it works

Pluck stays collapsed on the side of your screen.  
Click it to open a panel with your folders and saved images.

You can drag in screenshots, paste images, and organize everything however you want.

<!-- screenshot: folder view -->

When you open an image, you can keep it floating on top of your browser or other apps, so it stays visible while you work.

That way youâ€™re not constantly switching tabs just to check a design.

## What you can do

- Drag & drop images and screenshots  
- Copy and paste images in and out  
- Organize inspiration into folders  
- Keep design references floating on top of other apps  
- Use it as a lightweight, always-accessible design shelf

Open the project in Xcode, hit Run, and the app will launch as a native macOS app.
